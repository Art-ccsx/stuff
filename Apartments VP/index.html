ARCHITECTURAL CONCEPT

The architectural style of the GENERATION club house is an example
of an elegant and harmonious integration of modern trends into the
familiar urban landscape of the center of Bangkok. Current panoramic
glazing and the use of natural stone in the decoration form the basis of
a thin , organic matter that brings together a modern building
and a historical architectural ensemble.
